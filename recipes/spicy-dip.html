Spicy Peanut Dip

Description

This dip packs a protein and fiber punch with both peanut butter and white beans!

Ingredients

  • 15.5 oz can white beans, drained and rinsed
  • Juice of two limes
  • Â½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons unseasoned rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons garlic chili sauce

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Serve topped with a swirl of additional garlic chili sauce (optional)s
